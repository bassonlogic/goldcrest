Welcome to Goldcrest

Premier hunting in the heart of South Africa

The Ultimate Hunting Experience

Goldcrest Game Farm offers world-class trophy hunting across thousands of acres of pristine African bushveld. Our family-run farm has been cultivating exceptional game for generations, ensuring ethical, sustainable hunting practices and unforgettable experiences.

From the majestic kudu to the powerful sable, every hunt at Goldcrest is guided by experienced professionals who know the land intimately. We pride ourselves on responsible game management and providing hunts that challenge and reward.

Our Trophy Species

Six of Africa's finest game animals

Blue Wildebeest

The iconic African gnu, known for its distinctive appearance and challenging hunting. Mature bulls offer impressive trophy potential.

Kudu

The majestic greater kudu, prized for its magnificent spiraling horns. One of Africa's most sought-after trophies.

Springbok

South Africa's national animal, known for its speed and graceful pronking. A classic African plains game trophy.

Gemsbok

The striking oryx with rapier-like horns. Built for the harshest terrain, the gemsbok is as beautiful as it is tough.

Zebra

The iconic Burchell's zebra, with its unique stripe pattern. A striking trophy that embodies the African wilderness.

Sable

The magnificent sable antelope, with its scimitar-shaped horns and jet-black coat. The crown jewel of African antelope hunting.

Our Lodging

Comfort, hospitality, and the African wilderness

Premium Accommodation

After an exhilarating day in the field, return to the comfort of our lodge. We offer premium accommodation designed to provide the perfect blend of rustic charm and modern luxury. Each room is thoughtfully appointed to ensure your stay is as relaxing as it is memorable.

Our experienced staff takes pride in delivering exceptional hospitality. From the moment you arrive, you'll be treated to warm South African hospitality, gourmet meals prepared with fresh local ingredients, and attentive service that anticipates your every need.

Accommodation Options

The Main Lodge

Our flagship accommodation featuring four en-suite bedrooms, a spacious common area, and a large veranda overlooking the bushveld. Perfect for groups and families.

  • 4 en-suite bedrooms
  • Air conditioning & heating
  • Pool & outdoor lounge
  • Full board dining

Bush Chalets

Private, self-contained chalets situated along the dry riverbed. Each chalet offers total privacy with uninterrupted views of the surrounding wilderness.

  • Private deck with views
  • En-suite bathroom
  • Mini-bar & coffee station
  • Outdoor shower

The Hunting Camp

For the ultimate immersive experience, our tented hunting camp places you in the heart of the action. Canvas luxury under the African stars.

  • Meru-style canvas tents
  • Comfortable bedding
  • Campfire dining
  • Solar-powered lighting

What's Included

Gourmet Meals

Enjoy world-class South African cuisine prepared by our in-house chef. From traditional braais (barbecues) to elegant dinners, every meal is an experience.

Premium Beverages

Complimentary selection of South African wines, beers, spirits, and soft drinks. Enjoy a sundowner on the veranda after a successful day's hunt.

Professional Services

Full-time staff including housekeeping, laundry service, and butler service. We take care of everything so you can focus on the hunt.

Field Preparation

On-site skinning, caping, and meat preparation facilities. We ensure your trophies are properly cared for from the field to the taxidermist.

Airport Transfers

Convenient pickup and drop-off from the nearest airport. We handle all ground transportation to and from Goldcrest Game Farm.

Wi-Fi & Connectivity

Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the main lodge. Share your adventure with family and friends back home.

About Goldcrest

Our story, our passion, our promise

Our Heritage

Goldcrest Game Farm is a family-owned and operated hunting reserve, built on generations of respect for the land and its wildlife. Located in the heart of South Africa's prime hunting territory, our farm spans thousands of hectares of diverse terrain — from open plains to dense bushveld.

Our family has been stewards of this land for decades, carefully managing game populations and habitats to ensure sustainable hunting for years to come. Every animal taken on Goldcrest is done so in accordance with strict ethical guidelines and conservation principles.

What Sets Us Apart

Expert Guidance

Our professional hunters (PHs) have decades of combined experience. They know every inch of the farm and will ensure you have the best chance at the trophy you're after, while prioritizing safety and ethical practice.

Premium Game

We selectively manage our herds to produce exceptional trophy-quality animals. Our breeding programs focus on genetics, nutrition, and habitat to grow the finest specimens.

Conservation First

Ethical hunting is conservation. Our management practices ensure healthy, balanced ecosystems. Every hunt contributes directly to wildlife preservation and anti-poaching efforts.

Unforgettable Experience

From the moment you arrive, you're family. We provide world-class hospitality, exceptional meals, and comfortable lodging. Your safari with us is about more than the hunt — it's a complete African adventure.

Ethical Hunting

We believe in fair chase. Our hunts are conducted with the utmost respect for the animal and the environment. We do not permit unethical practices and ensure every hunt is a true test of skill.

Custom Safaris

Every hunter is different. We tailor each safari to your goals, fitness level, and experience. Whether you're a seasoned hunter or a first-time visitor to Africa, we'll craft the perfect hunt for you.
